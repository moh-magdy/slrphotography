About Us

Introduce About Us

We are an experienced marine photography company based in Hurghada, Egypt providing underwater services in photography and videography all over the Red Sea, one of the world’s most unique marine environments. The Red Sea is home to over 1200 species of marine life over 10% of which are not found anywhere else in the world. Our speciality is capturing diving and snorkelling experiences in action, along with this incredible marine life.

Whether you have dived or snorkelled before or just trying the experience for the first time, we’re at hand to capture those special moments, making it truly memorable. Alone or with family and friends we will capture those photos and videos in the water surrounded by the stunning marine life and coral reef or on board the well equipped boat – whatever your preference we will make it personal to you.

Our guests are our number one priority, assisting you with any needs you may have during your marine experiences here, making your stay extra special.
