Marine Biology

Marine biology covers a broad range of categories within all aspects of the marine environment. Whether you are a student in this field, have a career, an interest or passion for the underwater world in the Red Sea, we can assist you with any services you may require.
Marine Biology is our main focus as a company assisting you to ascertain evidence with the means of specialised underwater photographic and videographic equipment. We can gather this evidence on your behalf taking pictures to order or we can accompany you in your marine adventure, supporting you to achieve the photographs or video film you require



If you are an experienced diver we offer a PADI Certified Underwater Photographer course or PADI Certified Videographer Course. However, if you are not qualified we do offer the relevant courses as a package in order for you to achieve this. Please refer to our courses page for further details.
